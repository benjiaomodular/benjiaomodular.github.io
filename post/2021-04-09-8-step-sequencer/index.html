8-Step Sequencer

Fri, Apr 9, 2021

An 8-step sequencer based around the classic Baby-8 sequencer design. It utilizes a single CD4017 decade counter. Works on +9V power.

This works great! One possible issue with this is that the output isn’t quantized to the V/Oct standard so you’d have to tune each step yourself.
