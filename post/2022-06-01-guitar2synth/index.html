Guitar2Synth

Wed, Jun 1, 2022
+/-12v analog

Introduction

This knobless module is split into two parts. The guitar to synth section is a simple boost. The synth to FX section is a buffered attenuator which brings synth level (12V max) down to something resonable for guitar effects.

It is originally based on AI Synthesis' Stomp Adapter module.
