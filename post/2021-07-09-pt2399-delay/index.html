PT2399 Delay

Fri, Jul 9, 2021
+/-12v analog

I built this module initially as a guitar pedal, but decided to mount it on an acryllic panel for use with my modular. It’s based on DIY Guitar Pedals’s Boy in Well circuit.

I also later modded this one to add CV control on Time, Repeats, and Level.
