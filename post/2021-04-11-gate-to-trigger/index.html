Gate to Trigger

Sun, Apr 11, 2021
+/-12v 9v analog

This is a utility circuit for converting long gate signals to short, 7-10ms pulses at the leading edge.

I initially built this as a standalone module for use with my drum modules, but now I integrate this circuit into most of my new builds that need a trigger input.

Schematic

This design will also work on a 12V supply.
