About

I’m Benjie Jiao. I’m a Software Developer based in the Philippines.

During the lockdowns of late 2020 to 2021, I’ve taught myself electronics through building guitar pedals.

Eventually, I discovered I can generate sounds without a guitar using the same components as my pedal builds. This led me into a Synth DIY rabbithole. Since then, I’ve been adding modules to my homemade modular synth.

My friend Rex and I founded the local group Synth DIY PH for Filipinos who’d like to get into building modular synths. Message us on Facebook if you’d like us to invite you into our private Discord!

You can follow my progress on Youtube and Instagram.
